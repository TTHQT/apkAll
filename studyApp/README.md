# 📚 StudyApp — Your Pocket Study Buddy

> 🎯 This is an Android app I built by chatting with AI (Vibe Coding style). Free to use, free to share.

---

## 🤔 What Does It Do?

It's a **three-in-one study tool**: take notes → make quizzes → practice. Great for exam prep, flashcards, or just drilling knowledge.

### 📖 Knowledge Base — Take Notes
- Create **knowledge cards** (like digital flashcards with text)
- Organize into **groups** (e.g., "Math", "English", "History")
- **Full-text search** — type a keyword, find your card instantly
- Import **TXT or DOCX files** (Word docs) — AI can extract key points automatically
- **Merge groups** — combine them without manually moving cards one by one

### 📝 Exam Papers — Make Your Own Tests
- Create **exam papers** and add questions to them
- **5 question types**: Single Choice, Multiple Choice, True/False, Fill in the Blank, Short Answer
- Each question can be **linked to a knowledge card** — helpful when reviewing
- Use **AI to generate a full exam** in one click: pick your knowledge cards → choose question types → done

### 🧠 Quiz Mode — Practice and Review
- **3 quiz modes**:
  - ✅ Pick a specific paper
  - ✅ Mix questions from multiple papers (random pick)
  - ✅ Random questions from the entire question bank
- **Auto-save** your answers, **auto-grade** objective questions
- See **detailed results** with correct answers and explanations
- Each question shows its **linked knowledge card** for quick review

### 🌐 Bonus Feature
Built-in **HTTP server** — open `http://your-phone-ip:8080` on your computer browser (same Wi-Fi) and type questions on a real keyboard. No more tapping on a tiny screen.

---

## 📲 How to Install?

Download the APK and install it on your Android phone.

Minimum **Android 8.0** (phones from 2017 still work).

---

## 💡 Need AI Features?

The core features work **fully offline** — no internet needed.

AI features (auto-extract knowledge, auto-generate exams) require an **API Key** (currently supports DeepSeek — costs a few bucks and lasts a long time). There's a settings screen in the app to configure it.
